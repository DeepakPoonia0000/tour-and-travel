Verified Experience

An ethereal morning at Fushimi Inari that defies description.

Kyoto is a city that rewards the early riser. We began our ascent through the vermilion gates at 5:00 AM, and having the trail to ourselves as the sun broke through the cedar forest was spiritual. The balance between the hyper-modern Kyoto Station and the whispering bamboo groves of Arashiyama is a testament to Japanese urban planning and cultural reverence.

Verified Experience

Gastronomy like no other, though crowds can be daunting.

The kaiseki dinner we had in Gion was a choreographed masterpiece of seasonal flavors. While the major shrines were crowded, slipping into the smaller, nameless temples offered the "Zen" I was searching for. My advice: wander aimlessly for an afternoon, you'll find the soul of Kyoto in the quiet side streets.

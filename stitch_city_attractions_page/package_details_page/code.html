Golden Triangle Tour:
7 Days of Heritage

location_on Delhi • Agra • Jaipur
calendar_today All Year Round

A journey through India's imperial heartbeat, where history lives in every stone.

Experience the opulence of the Mughal Empire and the grandeur of Rajputana royalty. This meticulously curated itinerary takes you from the bustling markets of Old Delhi to the ivory-white splendor of the Taj Mahal, and finally to the pink-hued palaces of Jaipur.

Our experts have handpicked the finest experiences—exclusive sunrise entries, private palace dinners, and guided strolls through artisanal quarters—ensuring your odyssey is as authentic as it is luxurious.

"The sunset over the Amber Fort was a moment I'll never forget. Truly an editorial-level experience."

— Elena V., Travel Journalist

The Itinerary

1
Arrival & Immersion

Old Delhi's Timeless Pulse

Welcome to the capital. Begin with a private rickshaw ride through the narrow alleys of Chandni Chowk, followed by a serene afternoon at Humayun’s Tomb.

3
The Love Poem

Agra & The Taj Sunrise

Arrive in Agra. Our guides ensure a pre-dawn entry to the Taj Mahal to witness the white marble change from soft lilac to glowing gold. Evening visit to the Agra Fort.

5
The Pink City

Jaipur's Royal Splendor

Explore the City Palace and the Hawa Mahal. In the afternoon, a private artisan workshop for block printing in the old city's craft cluster.
